About Usu>

Every year, millions of tons of plastic enter the oceans primarily from rivers. The plastic afloat across the oceans – legacy plastic – isn’t going away by itself. Therefore, solving ocean plastic pollution requires a combination of stemming the inflow and cleaning up what has already accumulated. Wild Ocean, a non-profit organization, is developing advanced technologies to rid the world’s oceans of plastic.

Our Vision

The ocean is at the center of who we are. Even if you’ve never seen the ocean, you’re touched by it every single day. It produces half the air you and I breathe every day, the food we eat and the water we drink. This is what we’re fighting to protect. Because people and communities depend on the ocean every single day. I hope you’ll be just as inspired as we are to join us in that effort.

Our Mission

Wild Ocean is working with you to protect the ocean from today's greatest global challenges. Toghether, we create science-based solutions for a healthy ocean and the wildlife and communities that depend on it.
